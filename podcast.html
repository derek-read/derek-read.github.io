The Coach's Road Podcast is something I started with my classmate and friend Rick Schreiber. Through our podcast we wanted to explore the topic we were learning about in school more deeply. This has been a very great experience so far and has allowed me to connect with experts from all over the world and in different fields of sports. I have learned a lot so far about coaching, leading, teaching, and supporting. Our show's motto and a theme we constantly return to is Humans, Athletes then Players and this is something we try to live by as coaches of young athletes.

The motivation behind our podcast is a two fold. First we wanted to deepen our own understanding of the topics we learn about in school while getting practical ideas from experts that we can then put into our own practice. The other piece of our motivation is to provide interesting and insightful conversations for coaches all over the world in all sports. We all have something to learn from each other and that is something this show has provided for me, a chance to learn.

Moving forward with the show we hope to continue to grow and bring in new and exciting guests. We want to continuously improve and grow as people, hosts and coaches. Our learning is just getting started and we are not stopping anytime soon!
